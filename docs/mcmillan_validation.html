McMillan (1968) systematically overestimates Tc, especially for strong-coupling materials (λ > 1). The Allen-Dynes (1975) correction reduces this bias. Ferromagnets predict nonzero Tc because magnetic ordering (not captured by McMillan) destroys Cooper pairing.
