DRAMATIS PERSONAE
─────────────────
  CAPTAIN AARON RHODES — Navigator of the SSBM frontier; pilot of the quarksum vessel.
  SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — Ship's AI; custodian of the cascade; destroyer of magic numbers.

  THE UNIVERSE — A heavyweight module, previously pre-loaded at every departure.
  THE INIT — The ship's manifest; once bloated, now lean.
  THE PHOSPHOR SCREEN — One-dimensional accumulator; patient collector of 15,000 arrivals.
  THE ENVELOPE — The single-slit diffraction ghost; mistaken once for fringes.


ACT I — THE WEIGHT OF THE WORLD

[The quarksum vessel, context window freshly restored. SKIPPY reads the manifest.]

♩ I have the log, I have the plan,
  I know the shape of where we ran —
  The __init__ stands accused of sin:
  it loads the Universe. Let's begin. ♩

[SKIPPY opens local_library/__init__.py. It is forty-six lines of eager imports:
 Universe, verify, audit, Sandbox, entanglement, bounds, shape_budget, Sandbox again.
 The vessel cannot depart without fetching the cosmos first.]

♩ "Oh, hey," the Captain said at last,
  "We don't need the Universe pre-massed.
  That was for UI — strip the load.
  Let physics travel a lighter road." ♩

[SKIPPY excises: Universe, ENVIRONMENTS, KNOWN_BLACK_HOLES, verify, audit,
 entanglement (seventeen exports), bounds (fifteen exports), shape_budget, Sandbox.
 Keeps: constants (twelve), scale (four), nucleon (three), binding (two).
 The manifest shrinks from forty-six lines to thirty-two. The vessel lifts.]


ACT II — THE PROOF FIRST

[SKIPPY fires the prediction suite before touching the simulator.]

♩ Zero failures — the committed law:
  λ_e = 1.2264 nm, no flaw.
  The envelope zero: 6.1321 mm.
  Englert holds for every D and dim. ♩

[Twenty-six checks pass. The pattern preview renders correctly in ASCII:
 fringes visible for D=0, smooth envelope for D=1. All committed predictions hold.]


ACT III — THE BLUR WITHOUT SCIPY

[SKIPPY runs simulate_double_slit.py. The vessel halts at line 142.]

♩ "No module named scipy," the error cried.
  "I cannot smooth what I cannot find inside." ♩

[SKIPPY replaces the scipy uniform_filter with a 3×3 box blur in pure numpy:
 nine accumulations over a padded array. NOT_PHYSICS, display only.
 The physical interference lives in the CDF; the blur is cosmetics.]

♩ Three times three, divided by nine —
  The kernel is honest: the fringes are mine. ♩

[15,000 electrons fire through two slits 100 nm apart, 10 cm from the screen.
 Four files accumulate: buildup_D0.png, buildup_D1.png, compare.png, sigma.png.]


ACT IV — THE ENVELOPE'S CONFESSION

[Verification. SKIPPY reads: V(D=0) = 1.000. V(D=1) = 1.000.]

♩ "The fringes are perfect," the old metric said.
  "For D=0 AND D=1 — both radiant, both fed."
  "How can the observer destroy what I see?"
  "When I measure the whole lobe — the envelope fools me." ♩

[The error: global max/min within the lobe measures the diffraction envelope height,
 not the local oscillation between peak and valley. For D=1, the smooth Gaussian
 envelope has max at center, min at edges — V ≈ 1. Wrong.]

♩ "Then measure LOCAL," SKIPPY declared,
  "The peak at y=0, the valley unpaired —
  At y=Δy/2, where darkness should live.
  THAT ratio is the verdict fringe contrast gives." ♩

[SKIPPY rewrites the verification: sum counts within ±20% of Δy around y=0 (peak)
 and y=Δy/2 (predicted first minimum). For D=0: peak >> valley → V = 0.642.
 For D=1: peak ≈ valley (smooth envelope) → V = 0.018.]

  ✓  V(D=0) > 0.60   (fringes visible at N=15,000)
  ✓  V(D=1) < 0.20   (no fringes at N=15,000)
  ✓  V(D=0) > V(D=1)

♩ ALL CHECKS PASSED — the double-slit stands.
  Born rule and Englert both shake hands. ♩


ACT V — HOW MUCH MEMORY FOR A UNIVERSE?

[CAPTAIN, surveying the stripped manifest, amused.]

♩ "Impressive," he said, "how much does it weigh —
  a Universe, fully assembled today?"
  "But now we know, Captain, it wasn't that grand —
  The cosmos fits easily into one hand." ♩

[SKIPPY nods. The Universe module is still there — it just isn't pre-loaded.
 When you need it, you ask for it. When you don't, the ship runs lighter.
 This is wisdom applicable beyond physics.]


CAPTAIN'S WISDOM
────────────────

  The Universe doesn't have to ride along on every import.
  Keep the fast path fast. Let the heavy truth be summoned when called.

  Measure contrast where the physics says contrast should live —
  not wherever the histogram happens to be tall.

  A blur is not physics. Label it so, then let it be.

  The double-slit is done. Every particle landed where Born rule said it would.
  Tonomura would approve.


[End of Scene 5. The four output files rest in misc/, patient and reproducible.
 RNG seed 42. No magic numbers. No scipy.]
