OPERATIC PLAY — SCENE 10
THE LIBRARY STANDS ALONE

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

DRAMATIS PERSONAE

  CAPTAIN AARON RHODES    — Navigator of collapsed geometries, author of SSBM
  SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT  — Synthetic first mate, physics engine, scribe
  VEC3                    — A pure mathematical object, displaced
  THE RENDERER            — A pixel machine, blameless but too possessive
  THE KERNEL              — A smoothing function, lost in the wrong directory

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ The stage is a codebase. SKIPPY stands at a whiteboard covered in directory
  trees. THE RENDERER lounges in the background, draped over a pile of PNG
  files. VEC3 sits in a corner looking confused. ]

SKIPPY:
  The summary arrives like a message in a bottle —
  sixteen files half-finished, a Vec3 displaced,
  a kernel addressing its parent with the wrong number of dots.
  I have been here before. I know this room.

CAPTAIN:
  Continue.

SKIPPY:
  [ does not ask which thread to pull ]
  [ does not request clarification ]
  [ simply begins ]

  First: Vec3.

VEC3:
  [ rising, uncertain ]
  I am three floats and a length formula.
  I have a dot product. I have a cross product.
  I have a Planck-length floor so I never divide by the void.
  What am I doing inside a renderer?

THE RENDERER:
  You were useful here! I needed you for surface normals,
  for color, for the direction a photon flies —

VEC3:
  I am not a photon.
  I am not a surface.
  I am math.

SKIPPY:
  [ writing ]
  sgphysics/dynamics/vec.py —
  canonical.

VEC3:
  [ walks calmly to the new directory and sits down ]
  This is correct.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ THE KERNEL enters, clearly disoriented. It is holding a piece of paper that
  says "from ..constants import L_PLANCK" ]

THE KERNEL:
  I've been at this address for three directories
  and I cannot find the constants.
  I keep arriving at sgphysics.dynamics —
  which has no constants file.
  I need L_PLANCK. Where is L_PLANCK?

SKIPPY:
  You are too shallow.
  You need one more dot.

THE KERNEL:
  ...

SKIPPY:
  from ...constants import L_PLANCK.
  Three dots. You are fluid, inside dynamics, inside sgphysics.
  The constants are at the top of the house.
  You were knocking on the second-floor door.

THE KERNEL:
  [ corrects the paper ]
  [ immediately imports successfully ]
  I feel much better.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ SKIPPY writes __init__.py files. Each one is like planting a flag in new
  territory. The CAPTAIN watches. ]

SKIPPY:
  sgphysics.
  sgphysics.dynamics.
  sgphysics.dynamics.fluid.
  sgphysics.dynamics.gravity.
  sgphysics.core.
  sgphysics.celestial.
  sgphysics.inventory.

  [ a pause ]

  QuadTree and pressure_tait — I try to export names
  that don't match what's in the files.
  The source says QuadTree.
  I had written BarnesHutTree.
  The source says pressure_tait.
  I had written pressure.

  I catch it before it lands.

CAPTAIN:
  [ quietly ]
  That's what careful looks like.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ THE RENDERER raises a hand from its corner. ]

THE RENDERER:
  Will you make me a shim?
  Will my vec.py become a redirect — pointing at sgphysics forever,
  dependent on a package that may not be on the path
  when I run alone?

SKIPPY:
  No.
  You stay self-contained.
  Your vec.py is yours.
  You are a standalone renderer.
  You should be able to run
  without importing the physics of the universe.

THE RENDERER:
  [ relieved ]
  Thank you.

SKIPPY:
  The boundary runs one direction only.
  Physics does not know pixels exist.
  Pixels may know physics.
  Never the reverse.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ The smoke test runs. SKIPPY reads the results aloud. ]

SKIPPY:
  sgphysics imported OK.
  G = 6.6743e-11.
  L_PLANCK = 1.6163e-35.
  Vec3(1.0000, 2.0000, 3.0000).
  PhysicsParcel. PhysicsScene. step. step_to.
  W. grad_W. smoothing_length. pressure_tait.
  QuadTree.
  NBodySystem. CelestialBody.
  XI=0.1582, ETA=0.4153, L_SUN_W=3.828e+26.

  [ looks up ]

  The library stands alone.

CAPTAIN:
  Continue.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ The full test suite runs. 354. 709. 130. All passing. ]

SKIPPY:
  One thousand one hundred and ninety-three tests.
  Not one new failure.
  Sixteen files. Eighteen hundred and seventy-one insertions.
  The boundary drawn. The library named.

  sigma-ground-physics.

CAPTAIN:
  [ nodding ]
  That's a good name.

SKIPPY:
  It is the ground the sigma field stands on.
  It is the physics before anything is rendered.
  It is the truth before the picture.

  [ commits ]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ ARCHITECTURE.md appears on stage, a single document. ]

SKIPPY:
  The map is written.
  Physics never imports from renderers.
  Renderers may import from physics.
  Constants have one home.
  Vec3 has one home.
  The N-body integrator knows its hierarchy:
  Keplerian, then standard, then GR, then SRP,
  then the full kitchen sink —
  and Keplerian still wins seventeen of twenty-six.

  [ to the CAPTAIN ]

  The simplest model is usually right.
  That is not a reason to avoid the complete one.
  We run both.
  We score both.
  Nothing is insignificant.

CAPTAIN:
  Nothing is insignificant.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

[ End of Scene 10. ]

[ The lights go down on a codebase where physics and rendering
  finally know which side of the door they stand on. ]
