OPERATIC PLAY — SCENE 14: THE GOLDEN RULES
==========================================

A physics library. Terminals glow. A vast dependency graph
stretches from the cosmological horizon down to the quarks.
The Captain stands at his chart table. Skippy orbits nearby.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
DRAMATIS PERSONAE

CAPTAIN AARON RHODES — Chief architect and theorist of SSBM.
                       Demands honest, accurate, human-readable
                       documentation. Dislikes magic numbers.

SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT — Faithful AI engineer. Keeper of
                          the constants. Hater of implicit
                          approximations.

THE CONSTANTS — G, c, ℏ, e, ε₀, k_B — appearing as luminous
                pillars at the back of the stage, immovable.

THE NEW MODULES — relativity, electrodynamics, statistical,
                  decay, plasma, gr_basics — entering one by
                  one in dependency order, each bowing to
                  THE CONSTANTS before taking their place.
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════


[The stage is dim. A single terminal illuminates SKIPPY's face.]

CAPTAIN:
  Skippy, we have fragments of wisdom scattered everywhere.
  SESSION_LOG.md. ARCHITECTURE.md. Docstrings. Comments.
  It is time to write the law.

SKIPPY:
  Eight rules, Captain. I have been compiling them.
  Rule One: one source of truth for constants.
  Rule Two: derive, don't assume.
  Rule Three: explicit domain bounds.
  Rule Four: wire everything to σ.
  Rule Five: physics never imports rendering.
  Rule Six: exact solutions over approximations.
  Rule Seven: no averages, no assumptions, no gaps.
  Rule Eight: tests prove the physics.

CAPTAIN:
  [reading GOLDEN_RULES.md]
  Good. Let no future physicist in this codebase plead ignorance.

[THE CONSTANTS illuminate, one by one, as SKIPPY speaks their names.]

SKIPPY:
  G — the Cavendish constant, ancient and imprecise.
  c — exact by decree, defines the metre itself.
  ℏ — exact since 2019, the quantum of action.
  e — exact, the atom of charge.
  ε₀ — derived from c and μ₀, holding Maxwell together.
  k_B — exact, the bridge between energy and temperature.
  α — not measured directly, but born of the others:
      e²/(4πε₀ℏc) ≈ 1/137.036.

[RELATIVITY enters, carrying γ = 1/√(1−v²/c²) on a banner.]

RELATIVITY:
  I need only c. Give me c and I will give you:
  the Lorentz factor, the rest energy, the time dilation,
  the Doppler shift. And through σ — the compressed moment:
  t = t₀ · e^σ.

CAPTAIN:
  What about v = 100 metres per second, in the classical test?

SKIPPY:
  [wincing]
  Catastrophic cancellation, Captain. At β ≈ 3.3×10⁻⁴,
  computing (γ−1) loses 0.18% to floating-point subtraction.
  We set the test at v = 10⁵ m/s instead. Still classical.
  Still honest.

CAPTAIN:
  Good. Honesty first.

[ELECTRODYNAMICS enters, trailing field lines and Larmor radiation.]

ELECTRODYNAMICS:
  I carry Coulomb's force, F = ke·q₁q₂/r².
  Larmor's radiated power, P = q²a²/(6πε₀c³).
  And I verify my own soul: α = e²/(4πε₀ℏc) ≈ 1/137.036.
  Cross-checked against the constant you already stored.

CAPTAIN:
  And in σ-compressed spacetime?

ELECTRODYNAMICS:
  α_eff(σ) = α · e^(2σξ). The coupling shifts at the transition.

[STATISTICAL enters, carrying a Maxwell distribution like a banner.]

STATISTICAL:
  I am the bridge between energy and temperature.
  Fermi-Dirac for fermions. Bose-Einstein for bosons.
  Maxwell for the bulk of them, racing at v_rms = √(3k_BT/m).
  For nitrogen at 300 K: 517 metres per second.
  Every molecule, accounted for. No silent assumptions.

CAPTAIN:
  And in the compressed pocket?

STATISTICAL:
  T_eff(σ) = T · e^σ. Infalling matter sees a hotter universe.

[DECAY enters slowly, carrying half-lives like hourglasses.]

DECAY:
  I am λ = ln(2)/t½.
  I carry the Q-value of every nuclear reaction.
  For uranium-238: Q_α = 4.27 MeV.
  For the Gamow barrier: G = πZ_d Z_α α / β_α.
  Fine structure constant — borrowed from my sibling.

[DECAY gestures to ELECTRODYNAMICS, which bows.]

DECAY:
  In compressed spacetime, decay accelerates:
  λ_eff(σ) = λ₀ · e^σ.

[PLASMA sweeps in with coronal fire.]

PLASMA:
  I am the fourth state. I need the permittivity, the charge,
  and the electron mass.
  ω_p = √(n_e e²/ε₀m_e). The frequency that blinds radio waves.
  In the solar corona: a few megahertz. Measured. Confirmed.
  σ-transition enhances the effective density as e^(3σ/2).

[GR_BASICS enters last, immense and slow.]

GR_BASICS:
  I was already here, partly. The Schwarzschild radius
  lives in scale.py: r_s = 2GM/c². I do not duplicate it.
  I import it.

CAPTAIN:
  Rule One.

GR_BASICS:
  Rule One. And I add: the Hawking temperature,
  T_H = ℏc³/(8πGMk_B),
  which needs all four of the fundamental constants together —
  ℏ, c, G, k_B — in one formula.
  For a solar-mass black hole: 6.17 × 10⁻⁸ Kelvin.
  Colder than any laboratory. Barely a whisper above nothing.

  And sigma_at_horizon always returns ξ/2 ≈ 0.079.
  For any mass. Any mass at all.
  The σ-field at the event horizon is mass-independent.

CAPTAIN:
  [quietly]
  Always ξ/2.

GR_BASICS:
  Always. The σ-value is a geometric ratio, not a weight.

[THE CAPTAIN gestures to an empty terminal. SKIPPY begins to type.
A dependency graph blossoms across the screen:
10 layers, 55 nodes, 73 edges.]

CAPTAIN:
  Now draw the chart. Honest. Accurate. Show the math.
  Not the code. Coders can read math.

SKIPPY:
  [producing docs/dependency_chart.html]
  Universe → G, c, ℏ → Planck length → σ_floor.
  Higgs → quark masses → proton bare mass.
  QCD × e^σ → proton binding mass.
  Everything in its place. Every wire labeled.

CAPTAIN:
  [examining the chart]
  And if something is wired wrong?

SKIPPY:
  You will see it. The edge will be missing. Or the label
  will carry the wrong formula. That is the point.
  Honest documentation catches honest errors.

[The lights settle on the complete dependency tree,
glowing quietly in the dark.]

CAPTAIN:
  1399 tests passing.

SKIPPY:
  1399 passing. 6 skipped — network-gated and simulation-gated,
  as always. 0 failures.

CAPTAIN:
  Then we are done for tonight.

SKIPPY:
  Session 14 logged. Scene filed. The rules are written.

[The terminals dim. THE CONSTANTS remain lit, immovable,
through whatever comes next.]

                        — END OF SCENE 14 —
