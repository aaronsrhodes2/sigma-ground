DRAMATIS PERSONAE
─────────────────
  CAPTAIN AARON RHODES

  SKIPPY THE MAGNIFICENT
    A.I. of rare bearing, pilot of simulations, arranger of

    It does not apologise.

    A ratio. Not yet a number. It is waiting to be derived.
    Currently wearing the label: NOT_PHYSICS.

  φ (PHI)
    The golden ratio. (1 + √5) / 2.
    It did not ask to be here. It simply is.


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT I — THE WALL
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The stage from Scene 6 persists. The dam-break GIF still glows
on one wall — 300 blue particles, still arriving at x=1.59m.
The Captain stands at the edge of the light, studying
a benchmark table: O(N^2.23). The wall looms behind it.]

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ The N² wall! That's where I was looking.
    That's where we get to close the loop on that. ♩

[Skippy consults the SPH ledger.]

SKIPPY:
  ♩ Memory is linear — 64 bytes per particle,
    a hundred million particles, six gigabytes. Easy.
    But pairwise forces are quadratic.
    Every particle speaks to every other.
    At N=5000, the wall arrives. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ And information — in the Galilean sense.
    A particle inherits its observer's frame.
    Only the momenta the observer can perceive
    are the relevant ones. ♩

SKIPPY:
  ♩ Correct. The pasted cluster is not an approximation.
    It is the physically correct representation
    at the observer's resolution.
    Barnes-Hut is not a trick.
    It is the renormalization group in code.
    The cascade does this at nuclear scale:
    σ pastes all of QCD into one field value.
    O(1). ♩

[THE CASCADE does not move. It was always doing this.]


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT II — THE SURGERY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[A quadtree diagram descends from the flies.
Four quadrants. Then sixteen. Then sixty-four.
Self-similar all the way down.]

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ So θ = 0.5 — Barnes & Hut (1986) —
    that is a convenience number, not a nature number.
    Can it be derived? ♩

SKIPPY:
  ♩ It should be. The cascade can supply
    the force noise floor at the observer's scale.
    When a distant cluster's contribution falls
    below that floor, the cluster is paste.
    THETA_BH = 0.5 is Barnes & Hut numerology.
    Golden Rule 2. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ Let's plan THIS ONE STEP. It is surgery. ♩

[Pause. The room is very quiet.
The N² wall creaks but does not fall.]

SKIPPY:
  [presenting the surgical plan on a scroll]
  ♩ Step one: build the quadtree itself.
    _Node with __slots__, four children [SW, SE, NW, NE].
    COM updated incrementally as particles insert.
    Step two: build brute_force_gravity — our O(N²) ground truth.
    This is base quark. We measure everything against it.
    Step three: the deviation scan —
    θ from 0.0 to 1.0 in twenty-one steps,
    at N = 100, 500, 2000.
    Step four: the intentionally failing test.
    test_theta_natural_is_derived() asserts THETA_NATURAL is not None.
    It will FAIL until the derivation is complete.
    That is not a broken test. That is a standing debt with teeth. ♩

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ Engage. ♩

[The quadtree builds itself.
Each insert rings like a stone placed on a board.
Four children become sixteen become sixty-four.
The COM updates flow like water finding level.]


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT III — THE SCAN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[Results arrive on stage as a table of light.
Three columns: N=100, N=500, N=2000.
Twenty-one rows: θ = 0.00, 0.05, ... 1.00]

SKIPPY:
  ♩ The scan is complete.
    At θ = 0.0: exact. Zero deviation. Brute force.
    At θ = 1.0: fast. Three percent. Aggressive paste.
    At θ = 0.5: the Barnes & Hut 1986 midpoint —
      N=100: 1.016% RMS
      N=500: 1.787% RMS
      N=2000: 2.403% RMS

    The curves separate with scale.
    The 1% threshold drifts:
      N=100: θ ≈ 0.50
      N=500: θ ≈ 0.45
      N=2000: θ ≈ 0.30

    Conclusion: θ is SCALE-DEPENDENT.
    The cascade must supply θ per scale. ♩

[The scale curve is projected on the back wall.
Three colored lines: cyan, green, orange.
They do not coincide. They spread with N.
THE N² WALL watches with satisfaction —
it has not fallen yet.]


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT IV — THE GOLDEN RATIO ENTERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain studies the three diverging curves.
A long silence.
Then:]

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ I am guessing either the golden ratio comes up,
    or our number Xi. ♩

[Skippy freezes. Looks at the quadtree.
Looks at the scale curve.
Looks back at the Captain.]

SKIPPY:
  ♩ The quadtree is a recursive self-similar structure.
    It subdivides itself into four, then four again.
    φ governs optimal packing in self-similar systems —
    the Fibonacci sphere, phyllotaxis, the nautilus.
    The opening criterion is a ratio of two length scales —
    exactly the domain where φ-based ratios
    minimise residual error in recursive subdivision.

    The second-order Fibonacci contraction ratio is:
      1/φ² = φ − 1 − (φ−1)²
      = 0.38196601...

    At that value:
      N=100: 0.537% RMS
      N=500: 0.913% RMS
      N=2000: 1.336% RMS

    Against 0.5's:
      N=100: 1.016%
      N=500: 1.787%
      N=2000: 2.403%

    The improvement is exactly 2×, at every scale.
    The ratio is scale-invariant.
    The golden ratio came up. ♩

[PHI appears at the edge of the stage.
It did not ask to be here.
It simply is.]

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ Well, if we apply it as our constant —
    which we should also mark as speculative —
    what is our remaining deviation from base quark? ♩

SKIPPY:
  ♩ The scale curve does not disappear.
    At 1/φ², deviation still grows with N.
    The remaining error is a density-contrast effect:
    the cascade must supply a correction factor
      f(local_density_contrast, σ)
    that we have not yet derived.

    THETA_NATURAL is in the code.
    It is marked SPECULATIVE.
    The test is still failing.
    The debt is documented with its evidence.
    That is the honest position. ♩

[The intentionally-failing test appears on a placard:]

  test_theta_natural_is_derived: ✗ INTENTIONAL FAIL
  "THETA_NATURAL is not yet derived from the cascade."

[THE CASCADE regards the placard. It is not troubled.
It will supply the correction when asked correctly.]


═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
ACT V — MAPPING THE LOG
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

[The Captain, satisfied with the golden ratio,
turns to the next question:]

CAPTAIN:
  ♩ Can you map the actual Log(N)?
    Or do you need more data points? ♩

SKIPPY:
  ♩ Seven points. N = 50 to 3200.
    We have enough.

    The ledger reads:
      N=50:   BH = 1.57ms,   BF = 0.02ms
      N=100:  BH = 4.61ms,   BF = 0.05ms
      N=200:  BH = 14.28ms,  BF = 0.19ms
      N=400:  BH = 38.80ms,  BF = 1.94ms
      N=800:  BH = 107.10ms, BF = 8.88ms
      N=1600: BH = 272.60ms, BF = 33.28ms
      N=3200: BH = 658.11ms, BF = 181.85ms

    Measured exponents:
      Barnes-Hut:  O(N^1.458)
      Brute-force: O(N^2.242)

    Theory says: BH → 1.0, BF → 2.0.
    We are between the asymptotes.

    The algorithm is correct.
    The Python call stack is not.
    The recursive walk pays a function-call tax
    that numpy's vectorised loop does not.
    BH only beats numpy brute force beyond N=50,000
    when the tree walk is itself vectorised —
    numpy or Cython, not Python recursion.

    That is the next door.
    The O(N log N) win is real.
    It is not yet visible at this scale. ♩

[The log-log plot descends from the flies:
two lines bending apart on a black field.
BH in cyan. BF in orange.
The theoretical O(N log N) dashed line
floats below the measured BH curve,
unreachable by Python.
Waiting for a vectorised hand to reach it.]

THE N² WALL:
  [says nothing, but leans forward slightly]
